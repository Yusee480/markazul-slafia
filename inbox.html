Qur'an

Qur'an, (Arabic:"Recitation") Qur'an: The holy book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be the literal word of God as revealed to Prophet Muhammad.



Hadith

Hadith Collections (e.g., Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim): Compilations of sayings, actions, and approvals of Prophet Muhammad, serving as a secondary source of guidance in Islam.



Tafsir

Tafsir: Commentaries on the Qur'an, written by Islamic scholars to provide explanations, interpretations, and contextual insights into the verses.



Fiqh

Fiqh Books: (e.g., Reliance of the Traveller, Al-Muwatta): Islamic jurisprudence texts that detail the legal principles and guidelines for various aspects of Muslim life.



Seerah

Seerah Books: (e.g., "Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum" by Safi-ur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri): Biographies of Prophet Muhammad, offering a comprehensive account of his life, character, and historical context.
